The New Trend

Site Purpose

  • The reason I chose a subject of fashion is that it enables people to experiment with different styles, colors, patterns and accessories to create a unique and personalized look. The website will provides a platform to connect with customers , showcase products and drive sales.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

  • "I'm a new customer looking for information on how to place an order online."
  • Question: "How do I place an order on your website? Is it secure?"
  • Goals: Understand the website's ordering process, ensure security and trust.
  • Target audience: New customers, potential customers who have not yet made a purchase online.

Scenario 2

  • "I'm a returning customer who wants to track my order status."
  • Question: "How do I track my order status? Can I get updates on my shipment?"
  • Goals: Get timely updates on order status, track shipment progress.
  • Target audience: Returning customers, loyal customers who have made previous purchases online.
